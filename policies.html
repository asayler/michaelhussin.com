Policies and Rates

During the initial visit, I will listen to your main reason for coming, take a full health history, and listen to any questions or concerns you may have. If appropriate based on time remaining and evaluation of the presenting health issues, I can offer the first acupuncture treatment. Follow-up acupuncture sessions may include herbal prescriptions and suggestions for self-care. There is always ample time for further questions.

Normally the first visit is 60-75 minutes depending on the situation. Follow-up sessions are approximately 45 minutes. We use an adjustable fee schedule. The range is $75-$100 for the initial visit and $55-$75 for follow-up visits. If there are still concerns with payments you can speak with me directly.

I accept cash and checks, payable at the time of the visit. I do ask for at least 24 hours notice for cancellation and charge full fee for missed appointments.

Please call or leave a message at 413-584-9888 to schedule.

Insurance

Please check carefully with your insurance plan for current information. Some insurance companies cover acupuncture; many do not.

Automobile Personal Injury Protection (PIP) insurance will reimburse for acupuncture treatment directly related to auto accidents. We require payment at the time of your visit. We will provide you with the paperwork necessary to help with your reimbursement.

At this time, Medicaid and Medicare do not cover acupuncture.
